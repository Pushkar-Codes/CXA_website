Our Vision

At CODE X Artisans, in SRM University Sikkim, our vision is to instill a vibrant coding culture by offering hackathons, projects, and freelance work. We strive to empower students with practical coding skills and foster innovation through hands-on experiences. Our goal is to cultivate a community where members can collaborate, learn, and grow together. By providing opportunities for real-world application and professional development, we aim to equip our members with the expertise and confidence to excel in the rapidly evolving field of technology. At CODE X Artisans, we are committed to shaping the future leaders of the coding world.
